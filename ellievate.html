1 Foundation Phase

Establish Your Baseline

We begin with comprehensive assessment to understand your current leadership landscape, stakeholder perceptions, and strategic priorities.

  • 360° leadership assessment with stakeholder interviews
  • Psychometric profiling and leadership style analysis
  • Goal alignment session with executive sponsor
  • Personalized coaching roadmap development

Phase Outcome

Clear understanding of your leadership strengths, development opportunities, and a tailored 12-month growth plan.

2 Development Phase

Build Core Capabilities

Intensive skill-building focused on your highest-impact development areas. We work through real challenges in real time.

  • Bi-weekly 90-minute coaching sessions
  • Real-time application to live business challenges
  • Communication and influence skill development
  • Strategic thinking and decision-making frameworks
  • Between-session exercises and reflection tools

Phase Outcome

Measurable improvement in target leadership competencies, with observable behavior change in your daily executive practice.

3 Integration Phase

Embed New Behaviors

Shift from learning to mastery. We focus on sustaining change, expanding your impact across the organization, and building your coaching capacity.

  • Monthly coaching with on-demand support access
  • Team and stakeholder impact assessment
  • Cross-functional leadership opportunity execution
  • Peer coaching and mentoring skill development
  • Mid-program 360° progress review

Phase Outcome

New leadership behaviors are automatic and consistent. Expanded organizational influence and demonstrated business impact.

4 Sustainment Phase

Secure Long-Term Excellence

Transition to self-sustaining excellence. We ensure your growth continues independently and establish your legacy as a developing leader.

  • Quarterly check-ins and progress validation
  • Final comprehensive 360° assessment
  • Personal leadership philosophy documentation
  • Ongoing development plan for next 12 months
  • Alumni network access and continued resources

Phase Outcome

Sustained performance at elevated level. Clear trajectory for continued growth. Established as a coaching leader within your organization.
